Dr. Sagar Sangamesh Kamble

Dr. Sagar Sangamesh Kamble is an Ayurvedic practitioner dedicated to guiding health through nature. He completed his UG degree from KLE University, Shri BMK Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Belagavi, and pursued his MD from Sri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwar Ayurveda Mahavidyalaya, Udupi. He is currently pursuing a PhD from SDM College of Ayurveda, Udupi.

Currently, he serves as the Chief Consultant at Ayushman Health Care, Belagavi, and also works as an Assistant Professor at SBG Ayurveda Medical College, Belagavi, as well as a Consultant at SBG Ayurveda Hospital, Belagavi.

Dr. Chaitanya

Dr. Chaitanya is a highly skilled Ayurvedic practitioner who completed her degree from the esteemed Taranath Ayurveda Government College in Bellary.

With a strong background in women's health, she has gained valuable experience as a consultant in Gynecology and Obstetrics at RK Hospital, offering expert care and guidance to her patients.
